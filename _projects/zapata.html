This website was created for a Contemporary World History final project.

This was intended to be "project-based learning" - meaning I knew nothing about the Mexican Revolution beforehand.

Over the course of a month, I built this website from scratch.

I learned of Zapata's idealism, and violence, his complex relationships with other figures of the revolution, and his humble beginnings.
